How to Play
FIND, SELECT, AND LOAD A GAMEBEFORE INSERTING QUARTERS.

  1. 1
    Find your game Find game numbers on the Leaderboards Scan the QR Code Or browse the cabinet menu
  2. 2
    Select your game Move the joystick to highlight,
    Press 1UP to select.     Do not insert quarters yet.
  3. 3
    Wait for the game to load A loading screen appears for a few seconds. That is normal — not a glitch. Do not insert quarters until it finishes.
  4. 4
    Insert credits After the game loads, add credits (some $.25, others $.50).
  5. 5
    Play Press 1UP OR 2UP to begin playing.
  6. 6
    Back to the main menu Hold 1UP until the popup appears. Continue keeps playing Exit returns to the list.
  7. 7
    High score? We'll show it here Use the tablet to enter your name. Beat a champ — win a prize!
