Legends Arcade — TV celebration audio (5-part sequence)
=========================================================

Playback order:

  1. celebration.mp3              — Upbeat fanfare
  2. Congrats voice (ElevenLabs)
  3. [computer voice]           — player name only
  4. Leaderboard / #1 outro     — leaderboard.mp3 OR New High Score.mp3
  5. Winner outro.mp3           — final winner sting (always last)

Accepted filenames:

  Slot 1:  celebration.mp3
  Slot 2:  celebration-congrats.mp3  OR  Congratulations.mp3
  Slot 4:  celebration-outro.mp3     OR  leaderboard.mp3
           celebration-outro-top.mp3 OR  New High Score.mp3  (#1 only)
  Slot 5:  Winner outro.mp3          OR  winner-outro.mp3

Deploy to BOTH folders:
  services/arcade-events/static/tv/audio/
  FrontEnd/public/arcade-tv/audio/

Hard-refresh TV (Ctrl+F5). Use START-ARCADE-TV.bat for autoplay.
