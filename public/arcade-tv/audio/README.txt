Legends Arcade — TV high-score celebration audio
================================================

Drop your Upbeat (or other licensed) clip here:

  celebration.mp3

Specs that work well:
  - MP3 format
  - About 3–8 seconds
  - Upbeat announcer / game-show / “new high score” style
  - Loud and punchy (TV volume is often moderate)

The TV plays this clip when someone makes the leaderboard.
Player name still appears big on screen — the clip does not need to say their name.

If celebration.mp3 is missing, the TV falls back to built-in computer speech.

After adding or replacing the file:
  - Copy to both deploy locations (see arcade-events README), or
  - USB deploy: services/arcade-events/static/tv/audio/celebration.mp3
  - Production: FrontEnd/public/arcade-tv/audio/celebration.mp3
  - Hard-refresh the TV browser (Ctrl+F5)
